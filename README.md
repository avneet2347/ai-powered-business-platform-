# AI-Powered Business Intelligence Platform

## Project Overview

The AI-Powered Business Intelligence Platform is designed to help organizations transform raw business data into actionable insights. The platform integrates data from multiple sources, processes it through ETL pipelines, stores it in a structured data warehouse, and provides KPI dashboards, sales forecasting, and AI-driven recommendations.

## Objectives

* Track key business KPIs
* Monitor sales and revenue performance
* Forecast future sales trends
* Generate automated business insights
* Support strategic decision-making

## Features

### Data Warehouse Design

Structured schema for storing customer, product, and sales data.

### ETL Pipeline

* Data Extraction
* Data Transformation
* Data Loading

### KPI Dashboard

* Total Revenue
* Total Sales
* Customer Analytics
* Product Performance

### Forecasting

Revenue forecasting using historical sales data.

### AI Insights

Automated business recommendations based on sales trends.

### Cloud Architecture

Designed for deployment on AWS services.
